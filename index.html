About

Introduction

Hi, I'm Timothy! I'm an aspiring front end web developer. I'm currently working on front-end of things with a focus in JavaScript. In the future, I want to pursue full-stack knowledge in the MERN stack (Mongo, Express, React, and Node).

I participate in many groups including Chingu-Voyage, Reston Creative Social, and Free Code Camp Leesburg. In the past, I have worked remotely with a team with Chingu-Voyage, worked with clients in my freelance web design business, and designed in Wordpress with Divi Theme.

I love traveling, meeting new people, and snowboarding in my spare time. I am professional, punctual, and organized. Thanks for visiting and have a great day!
